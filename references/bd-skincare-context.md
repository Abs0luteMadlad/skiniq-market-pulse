# Bangladesh Skincare Market — Agent Context Reference

This file provides standing context that the SkinIQ Market Pulse agent
should use when generating reports. Load this when producing any analysis
specific to the Bangladesh cosmeceutical market.

---

## Market Overview

- Bangladesh skincare market estimated at $180M+ (2025), growing at ~12% CAGR
- Urban consumers (Dhaka, Chattogram) are primary premium segment
- Gen Z and millennial women (18–35) are highest-growth consumer segment
- Key purchase channels: pharmacy chains, cosmetics shops, online (Daraz, Shajgoj)

## Key Local Players
- **Bio-Xin Cosmeceuticals** — science-backed positioning, clinic + retail
- **Square Toiletries** — mass market, wide distribution
- **Kohinoor Chemical** — legacy brand, mid-market
- **Meril** (ACI) — strong offline distribution

## Key Imported Brand Competitors
- Garnier (L'Oreal Bangladesh)
- Himalaya
- Neutrogena
- Pond's (Unilever)
- COSRX, Some By Mi (Korean imports, growing online)

## High-Demand Ingredients (2025–2026)
- Niacinamide (brightening) — #1 trending ingredient
- Hyaluronic acid (hydration)
- SPF/sunscreen — rapidly growing category
- Salicylic acid (acne — growing Gen Z demand)
- Tranexamic acid — emerging

## Regulatory Body
- **BFDA** (Bangladesh Food Safety Authority) oversees cosmetics
- **DGDA** (Directorate General of Drug Administration) for cosmeceuticals
- Import registration required for foreign cosmetics

## Seasonality
- Hot humid season (April–September): SPF, oil-control, lightweight formulas
- Winter (November–February): moisturisation, barrier repair
- Eid periods: gifting, premium SKU spikes (2× per year)

## Consumer Behaviour Notes
- TikTok and YouTube are primary discovery channels for skincare
- "Glass skin" aesthetic strongly influences purchasing
- Price sensitivity is high outside premium segment
- Dermatologist endorsement carries significant credibility
- "Cosmeceutical" positioning (between cosmetic and pharmaceutical) is
  trusted differentiator vs mass market

## Strategic Intelligence Priorities (for report framing)
1. Any regulatory changes from BFDA/DGDA
2. Korean/international brand distribution expansion
3. New local brand launches or funding rounds
4. Ingredient trend movements
5. E-commerce platform promotions (Daraz 11.11, etc.)
